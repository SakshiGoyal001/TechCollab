Multi-Camera and AI Based Facial Attendance System

Description:

Utilized OpenCV and experimented with diverse facial recognition algorithms such as VGG and Resnet, to achieve robust and efficient attendance tracking. Integrated multiple camera feeds, employed image preprocessing techniques, and applied deep learning models to accurately identify and record student attendance using JETSON nano. Technology Used - OpenCV, Deep Learning(MTCNN, VGG16 and Resnet), JETSON. Researched about different Deep Learning Algorithm to optimize the real time crowd management. Led 6 students, partnered with professors, improved accuracy via ensemble ML models.
